artista

build web pages the way you'd build slides, native on mac

still in the early stages · from june 2026

a native mac app for building web pages by dragging stuff around — kind of like making slides, except the canvas is real html the whole time.

every element you place is an actual dom node. when you export, an llm reads your layout and turns it into clean, responsive html/css. works both ways too — paste in html from chatgpt or claude, tweak it visually, export it back out cleaner. you can also just tell the ai what to change in plain words and watch the canvas update.

bring your own api key (six providers supported), everything stays on your machine. built in swiftui + webkit.
