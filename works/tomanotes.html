tomanotes

a desktop notes app i shipped, somehow people actually found it

from march 2026

a free, open-source, local-first note app for mac. built it to pair with notiee (my ios photo-notes app) — they share the same .tmn file format so notes move between them without losing anything.

two editors in one: rich text (tiptap) or plain markdown, whichever mood you're in. notes can be locked behind touch id and encrypted one by one. five themes, three languages, everything stays on your machine.

mit licensed. code's on github.
