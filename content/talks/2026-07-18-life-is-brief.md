<!-- zh -->

标定向北向西的列车在蒸气消散后也不知所踪

但生活长，慢，悠且真，并总被无向性与未知所填满。

将浪漫化的修饰拂去，这好像便是生活所固有的模样。

若走着线性的道路，过着定性的美好生活。

人又是否会拥有真正的幸福？

经验与先验的，感性与理性的都不是决定性的

参照物，好像只有人的感受是最真实的。

缓慢前行，惊喜与小确幸总会在某个瞬间划过。

既然，知道这一点，就要勇敢地走下去。

就像在喘息时不停念叨的那样，

要保持振动，努力呼吸。

活下去，世界在我沉沦之前毁灭，留真我。

<!-- en -->

Trains marked northbound and westbound vanish without a trace after the steam disperses.

Yet life is long, slow, unhurried, and real, always filled with directionlessness and the unknown.

Strip away the romantic embellishment, and this seems to be life's inherent shape.

If one walks a linear path and lives a well-defined good life,

would one possess true happiness?

Neither experience nor the a priori, neither sensibility nor reason, is decisive.

It seems that the only point of reference is what a person feels, and that alone is truly real.

Move forward slowly. Surprises and little moments of happiness will always flash by.

Since I know this, I should keep walking bravely.

Just as I keep murmuring while catching my breath:

keep vibrating. keep breathing.

Live on. Let the world be destroyed before I sink; let the true self remain.
